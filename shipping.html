Delivery Times

Duties & Taxes

European Union

All orders shipped within the European Union include VAT and there are no additional customs duties or import taxes.

Non-EU Countries

For shipments outside the EU, local customs duties, taxes, and fees may apply. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our prices or shipping costs.

Important Note:

We cannot predict what these charges will be as they vary by country. For more information, please contact your local customs office.
